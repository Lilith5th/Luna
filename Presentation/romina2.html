The Poop Business

MOOD integrates a 99.9% closed-loop waste management cycle:

  1. Organic waste from plants (non-edible plant parts)
  2. Organic waste from insects (excrements, dead material)
  3. Organic waste from humans:
    • Solids
    • Urine
    • Menstrual fluid
  4. Greywater

Component overview of menstrual fluids:

Ratio: approximately 1/3 blood and 2/3 other substances

  • Water
  • Organic tissue
  • Sodium
  • Potassium
  • Electrolytes
  • Bacteria

Aeroponic Tower Garden - All-Plant Diet Guide

Leafy Greens

  • Lettuce (Romaine, Butterhead, Leaf lettuce): High in vitamins A and K, folate.
  • Spinach: Rich in iron, calcium, vitamins A, C, and K.
  • Kale: Packed with vitamins A, C, and K, and antioxidants.
  • Swiss Chard: Contains vitamins A, C, and K, magnesium.
  • Arugula: High in calcium, potassium, folate, vitamins C and K.
  • Mustard Greens: Rich in vitamins A, C, and K, fiber.

Herbs

  • Basil: Contains vitamin K, manganese, essential oils.
  • Cilantro: High in vitamins A, C, and K, antioxidants.
  • Parsley: Rich in vitamins A, C, and K, iron, folate.
  • Mint: Contains vitamin A, iron, and antioxidants.
  • Thyme: High in vitamin C, vitamin A, fiber.
  • Oregano: Rich in antioxidants, vitamins K, and E.

Vegetables

  • Tomatoes (cherry and small varieties): High in vitamins C, K, potassium.
  • Cucumbers (compact or bush varieties): Good source of hydration, vitamins K, and C.
  • Peppers (bell peppers, jalapenos): Rich in vitamins A, C, and B6.
  • Zucchini (compact varieties): Contains vitamin A, manganese, vitamin C.
  • Eggplant (small varieties like Fairy Tale): High in fiber, vitamins B1 and B6, manganese.
  • Radishes: Contains vitamin C, potassium, and fiber.

Fruits

  • Strawberries (everbearing varieties): High in vitamin C, manganese, antioxidants.

Additional Options

  • Microgreens (broccoli, radish, sunflower): Rich in vitamins C, E, and K, and antioxidants.
  • Edible Flowers (nasturtiums, violas): Provide vitamins C and A, antioxidants.

Nutritional Balance

  • Proteins: Spinach, kale, Swiss chard
  • Vitamins: Lettuce, tomatoes, bell peppers
  • Minerals: Parsley, basil, cilantro
  • Fiber: Cucumber, zucchini, radishes
  • Antioxidants: Strawberries, kale, oregano

Growing Tips for Aeroponic Towers

  • Lighting: Ensure 12-16 hours of light exposure daily, using natural light or artificial grow lights.
  • Nutrient Solution: Use a balanced nutrient solution formulated for aeroponic systems.
  • pH Levels: Maintain a pH level of 5.5-6.5 for optimal nutrient absorption.
  • Humidity and Temperature: Keep the environment within 65-75°F (18-24°C) and maintain moderate humidity.

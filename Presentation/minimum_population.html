Minimal Population for Lunar Base Food Production

Agricultural Technicians and Horticulturists

  • 3-4 experts in plant science and aeroponics

Engineers and Technicians

  • 3-4 engineers for system maintenance and troubleshooting
  • 1-3 IT specialists for managing automated systems and data analysis

Support Staff

  • 1-3 medical staff for health and emergencies
  • 1-3 general support personnel for logistics, food preparation, and other daily needs

Management and Coordination

  • 1-3 managers to oversee operations, schedule maintenance, and ensure smooth workflow

Research and Development

  • 1-3 researchers to innovate and optimize food production

Total Minimal Population

The total minimal population, is approximately 13-20 people. This group would be sufficient to manage and maintain an efficient, small-scale food production system in a space base, assuming high levels of automation and well-designed support infrastructure.

Aeroponic Tower Calculation

Monthly Production Per Tower: 8 kg

Daily Production Per Tower:

8 kg / 30 days ≈ 0.26 kg/day

Daily Caloric Requirement:

1,000 people × 2,000 calories/person/day = 2,000,000 calories/day

Total Food Production Needed (in kg):

2,000,000 calories/day / 300 calories/kg = 6,667 kg/day

Number of Aeroponic Towers Needed:

6,667 kg/day / 0.26 kg/tower/day ≈ 25,642 towers

Habitat Structure

Single Large Inflatable Habitat

  • Pressurized at atmospheric pressure: This allows Earth-like construction and living conditions. Maintaining an internal pressure equivalent to Earth's sea level reduces the complexity and cost associated with building in a vacuum or low-pressure environment. It also enhances crew comfort and safety.
  • Internal Modules: These modules are designed for living, research, recreation, and utilities. Each module maximizes efficiency and functionality with dedicated spaces for sleeping, working, relaxing, and maintaining the habitat's systems.

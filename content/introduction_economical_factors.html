Technological Advancements

Private investors are transforming the field with investments in satellite communications, launch services, and space tourism. Companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, and Virgin Galactic are leading innovations, increasing rocket payload capabilities significantly over the past decade.

Key Technological Developments:

  • Increased rocket payload capability: 15-20t to 60-100t LEO
  • Focus areas: research, production, energetics, health, and defense

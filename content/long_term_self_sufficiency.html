Long-Term Self-Sufficiency

Sustainable Practices

  • Utilization of in-situ resources: For construction and daily operations, reducing dependence on resupply missions from Earth.
  • Advanced recycling systems: Minimize waste and maximize resource use, essential for maintaining a closed-loop ecosystem.
  • Hydroponic gardens supported by bioreactors: Provide food and oxygen, creating a self-sustaining cycle within the habitat.

Community and Well-being

  • Facilities and programs to support mental health and social well-being: Ensuring a positive and supportive community environment.
  • Cultural and recreational activities: Maintain a vibrant community, helping to alleviate challenges of isolation and monotony.

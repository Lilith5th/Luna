Use Scenarios

Civil Use

  • Long-term habitation for scientists and engineers conducting research and experiments.
  • Development of sustainable living practices that can be applied to Earth.

Tourism

  • Facilities to accommodate space tourists seeking unique experiences.
  • Recreational and educational programs to enhance the tourist experience.

Exploration

  • Base for conducting lunar surface explorations and geological studies.
  • Launching point for missions to further destinations such as Mars.

Defense

  • Strategic location for monitoring and protecting space assets.
  • Coordination center for space defense operations.

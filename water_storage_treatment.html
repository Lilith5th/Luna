Water Storage and Treatment

Distributed Water Storage

  • 27 interconnected tanks: Each with 50,000 liters capacity. Distributed throughout the habitat for redundancy to minimize risk of a single point of failure.
  • Tanks: Ensure redundancy so that if one tank fails, others can continue to provide the necessary supply, reducing the risk of a complete water supply disruption.

Advanced Filtration and Recycling

  • Multi-stage purification: Reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, nano-filtration, activated carbon to ensure clean, safe drinking water.
  • Grey and black water recycling: Bioreactors for hydroponic gardens to reduce need for constant resupply from Earth and support food production.

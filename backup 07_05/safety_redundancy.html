Safety and Redundancy

Multiple Storage Tanks

  • 27 smaller tanks: Each 50,000 liters, interconnected for redundancy. Enhances the overall resilience of the habitat's water infrastructure.

Backup Systems

  • Redundant filtration units and comprehensive emergency protocols: Ensure that there are backups in place for critical systems and clear guidelines for responding to emergencies.

Emergency Procedures

  • Detailed protocols and regular training drills: Prepare the crew to respond effectively to various scenarios, ensuring safety and security.
